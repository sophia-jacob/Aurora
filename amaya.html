Amaya

Partner: Bryan
Birth Sign: Scorpio
Age: 25
Job: Cardiac Nurse
Hometown: New York City

Amaya Espinal, a 25-year-old cardiac nurse from New York City, joined Love Island USA Season 7 as a late arrival and quickly made an impression. While her entrance brought some early tension, she stood out for her honesty, confidence, and the way she handled challenges with grace. Her ability to stay true to herself helped her build meaningful connections in the villa.

She became especially popular after singing a spontaneous, heartfelt song in the makeup room, which went viral for its sincerity and humor. Fans connected with her playful personality, supportive energy, and her now-iconic nickname, “Amaya Papaya.” Her one-liners and positive attitude made her a bright spot in the season.

Amaya eventually formed a strong bond with Bryan Arenales during Casa Amor. Their connection grew naturally, leading them to win the season as the first Latino couple to take home the $100,000 prize. Their relationship continued after the show, with both expressing excitement about the future together.
